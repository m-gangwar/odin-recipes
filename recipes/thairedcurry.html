Vegan Thai Red Curry Recipe

Description

This recipe is for vegan thai red curry that is packed with vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Red Curry Paste
  • Coconut Milk
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Tofu
  • Thai Basil

Steps

  1. Mince garlic
  2. Fry garlic for 30 sec. and mix in 1 tbsp red curry paste
